Special Session on Trustworthy and Responsible Data Mining (DMSP 2025)

Organized by Dr. Chenxi Qiu, Dr. Primal Pappachan, and Dr. Li Yan

Abstract

The increasing reliance on data mining techniques across various domains necessitates a strong emphasis on trustworthiness and responsibility. This special session aims to explore cutting-edge methodologies, challenges, and solutions related to ensuring responsible and ethical data mining practices...

Aims and Scope

  • Fairness-aware data mining techniques
  • Transparency and interpretability in data mining
  • Privacy-preserving data mining methods
  • Secure multi-party computation for data mining
  • Federated learning and differential privacy
  • Adversarial attacks and defenses in data mining
  • Blockchain-based security mechanisms
  • Case studies and practical applications of responsible data mining
